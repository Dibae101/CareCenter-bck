KYAKAMEENA CARE CENTER

WHO ARE WE

We are a team of highly experienced professionals who give first rate care and personal service to those needing short-term or long-term care.

OUR MISSION

We provide a personalized plan of care with the physician to provide Nursing and Rehab Services to help our patients transition from the hospital to their home.

WHAT WE DO

We serve our clients with Post Acute rehabilitation and nursing services with the focus on helping our patients transition from the hospital to home.

Our Story

Elmwood Nursing and Rehabilitation Center is proud to have been serving the San Francisco East Bay area since 1965. Elmwood is committed to high quality patient care and satisfaction.

Often the transition to a skilled nursing facility is challenging. At Elmwood Nursing and Rehabilitation Center, we strive to ease the process by providing an environment where patients and guests feel informed and comfortable.

In addition to nurses and staff who are always available to answer questions and lend a hand, each patient is paired with a coordinator, to help ease transition and provide a familiar smiling face.

A team of qualified professionals helps patients recover from surgery, injury or serious illness. They understand the importance of creating a comfortable and nurturing atmosphere, whether patients stay for short-term treatment or long-term care.
